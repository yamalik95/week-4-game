The Divine War
User first selects preferred prophet, then battles infidel prophets. Safe is guaranteed damage to infidel. Risky is equal probability of no damage or twice the 'safe' damage to infidel. Insane is equal probability of either self-harm or infidel damage with 4x 'safe' damage. Each attack boosts 'safe' damage value. Lower base health means higher 'safe' damage and increment.
